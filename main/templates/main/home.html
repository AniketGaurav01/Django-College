About Us

Alliance University was founded with an ambition to serve society with education and research and promote a global outlook that defines the future trajectory of its students. Since its inception in 2010, Alliance University has been a forerunner in higher education, calling out to the new generation of scholars, engineers, scientists, artists, people in business, social reformers helping to shape and transform societies and communities through and beyond, around the world.
